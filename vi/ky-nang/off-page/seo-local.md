# seo-local — Local SEO

Local SEO cho doanh nghiệp có địa điểm vật lý: GBP, NAP, citations, reviews, local schema.

## Chức năng
- Google Business Profile optimization
- NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency check
- Citation presence audit
- Review health analysis
- LocalBusiness schema validation
- Location page quality (multi-location)
- Industry-specific local signals
