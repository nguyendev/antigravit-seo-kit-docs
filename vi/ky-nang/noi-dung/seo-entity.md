# seo-entity — Entity & Knowledge Graph

Entity NER (Named Entity Recognition), salience scoring, Knowledge Graph readiness audit.

## Chức năng
- Trích xuất named entities từ nội dung
- Đánh giá entity salience
- Kiểm tra Knowledge Graph presence
- Validate sameAs links (Wikipedia, Wikidata, social profiles)
- Schema.org entity markup recommendations
