# seo-geo — Tối Ưu AI Search (GEO)

Generative Engine Optimization: tối ưu website cho AI search engines (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Bing Copilot).

## Chức năng
- AI crawler detection (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, OAI-SearchBot)
- llms.txt standard detection
- Passage-level citability scoring (optimal 134-167 words)
- Brand mention signals (3x quan trọng hơn backlinks cho AI visibility)
- Platform-specific optimization
- Server-side rendering checks cho AI crawler accessibility
